– Endings –

Night of the Nun is such an immersive game, the developers created multiple endings for the player to experience. After playing the game and doing further research I have compiled a list of each ending.

Ending A: Mrs. McDonnell goes insane and buries herself alive.

Ending B: Mrs. McDonnell gets trapped in a reality-bending vent and is killed by a small humanoid figure made of light.

Ending C: Mrs. McDonnell slays the Nun by burning her alive, and is forgiven by Bongo the Clown.

Ending D: Mrs. McDonnell gets herself trapped in a room that the Nun fills with her acidic vomit, killing her.

Non-standard ending: Mrs. McDonnell gets herself trapped in a barbed wire pit, and the Nun arrives moments later, killing her.
