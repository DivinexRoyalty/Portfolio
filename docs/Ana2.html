– Characters and Items –

The game invites you to play as the mother of the daughter who was sent to the boarding school. You learn who the characters by playing the intro of the game where you start out in a bathtub and recieve a phonecall that your daughter is sick and needs to be picked up. We never meet the daughter in the game however, we get a very close interaction with the Nun. She vigorously chases you around the school in order to murder you before you can (A) kill her or (B) rescue your daughter from the school.

Although there are no NPC's in the game, yo uhave multiple items that you are able to use in order to reach one of the multiple endings of the game.
