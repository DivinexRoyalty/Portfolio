– I LOVE WHAT I DO –

Residing in the DMV area, SHM tha Artist uses his innovation to create remarkable pieces. There’s more depth to his work then at first glance. He started developing his own style and sharing it online at the age of 15. Over the years, he has gained an audience and has slowly built on his social media platforms. On Instagram he shares behind-the-scenes pictures and showcases the final artwork. I AM SHM mainly creates personal artworks, however recieves the opportunities to work with like-minded groups. He has previously worked with clients such as Overtime and HIRSHHORN.
